Who are OCEANIA?

We are a Non-Profit, NGO based in the USA, established in the year 2000, with offices worldwide mainly dedicated to save and restore our beautiful oceans. Since its founding, Oceana has won more than 200 victories and protected more than 4.5 million square miles of ocean. Aside from our work on oceans, we also aim to save the environment in any way possible, by scheduling beach cleanups, sea cleanups, etc.. No organization was working exclusively to protect and restore the oceans on a global scale. To fill the gap, Oceania was formed: we are an international organization focused solely on oceans, while being dedicated to achieving recognizable change by conducting specific, science-based policy campaigns with fixed deadlines and articulated goals.
