What We Do

We are Oceania, named after the continent, our mission is to do our best to save the world's oceans and in turn the rest of the Environment. Our main priority is saving the Ocean, since ocean ecosystems are responsible for creating 80% of the Oxygen we Breathe. However, we do not only aid the ocean, we also aid any environmental crisis we have the power to! All Environmental issues are interrelated; Ocean acidification is due to global warming, which is due to climate change, etc..

  3. More than 1 million species are at risk of extinction by climate change
  4. Climate change is already happening, and it’s detrimental to human life, too
