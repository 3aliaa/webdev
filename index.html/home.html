Welcome to Oceania Co.'s Official Website

We are a Non-Profit, NGO dedicated to saving the world's Oceans, which cover 70% of the total area of our planet Earth.

The Importance of the Ocean

  • Oxygen: The ocean is responsible for more than 50% of Oxygen Production.
  • Climate regulation: The Ocean absorbs Heat and transports it form the equator to the North & South Pole.
  • Medicine: a large amount of different medicinal products originate from the ocean, including ingredients that help fight cancer, athritis, Alzheimer's disease, and heart disease.
